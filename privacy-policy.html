Privacy Policy for MovieMapper

Last updated: January 7, 2025

Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Display name (for leaderboards)
  • Game statistics and progress

Usage Data:

  • Game sessions and scores
  • Search queries for movies and actors
  • Device information for app performance

How We Use Information

  • Game Features: Provide personalized game experiences
  • Leaderboards: Display competitive rankings
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage to enhance gameplay

Information Sharing

We do not sell your data
  • Firebase: We use Google Firebase for authentication and data storage
  • TMDB: Movie data is sourced from The Movie Database (TMDB)

Data Security

Your data is protected using industry-standard encryption and security measures provided by Firebase.

Your Rights

  • Access: View your stored data
  • Delete: Request account deletion
  • Update: Modify your profile information

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the app:

  1. Open Game Modes & Filters from the main screen
  2. Tap "Delete Account" at the bottom
  3. Confirm your choice through the verification steps

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your profile information and email
  • All game statistics and history
  • Your leaderboard entries
  • Any stored preferences

Important: This action cannot be undone. Account deletion is processed immediately and your data cannot be recovered.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@moviemapper.com
